You followed the villagers out of the city...

You go with them, and search for anyone who is willing to help.

Eventually, with the group of farmers and villagers you manage to gather, you travel out towards the mountains, where several

mysterious fires

had been set.

You saw the destruction on your journey...

Ash and smoke covered the farmlands, as well as the surrounding forests.

You could still feel the warmth of the few flames that remained.

The farmers you travel with tell you that the fires have been getting closer to the city...

As they do, you hear a loud thunderous sound above you...

Looking up, you search the sky for the source of the sound.

And there in the distance you see..

...A Dragon Approaching!

There isn't much time until it reaches you, and then the Kingdom!

Will You Try Stop The Dragon And Save The Kingdom? Or Will You Run?
