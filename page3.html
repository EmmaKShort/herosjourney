Your adventure begins!

You set out to travel the land, seeking to become a great adventurer.

On your travels, armed with only an old sword to protect you, you venture out on your own.

Your path takes you through local towns, as well as the forests that surround them.

After gaining some experience, you decide to head towards the main city of the Kingdom.

Along the way...

You come across a small village, on the outskirts of the city, surrounded by farmland.

However, as you approach the village, you begin to notice a large group of people running away in random directions.

Not far behind them, a large creature appears!

You follow the villagers, before finally catching up to one group.

The older man approaches you, asking for your help in stopping the creature.

Will You Help The Village Guide The Monster Away? Or Will You Fight It?
