You've stopped the monster!

After saving the small village, the villagers approach you.

They thank you for your help, gifting you with a new sword and shield.

Taking your gifts, you leave the village and finally arrive in the city.

As time passes, tales of your heroism spread throughout the city.

You help the city's people, taking on quests and fighting monsters along the way!

It isn't long until the Kingdom recognizes you as a skilled adventurer, and come to you for help.

It wasn't too long until one of the King's Knights approached you.

The knight announces that the King is gathering a group of adventurers for a quest!

An opportunity like this would allow you to become one of the most well-known adventurers in the Kingdom!

However, at the same time..

You notice the same man from the village you saved, talking to a group of farmers.

You listen to their conversation, and learn that many of the fields on the far side of the kingdom have been destroyed.

The farmers point out that the fields were all burned

and that fires have been spreading out towards The City.

The villager suggests that a group should go out and find the cause of these fires, before it gets closer.

Will you join and help the troubled villagers? Or will you ignore them and see the King?
