You ignored the villagers

You join the Knight, who takes you to the castle to see the King.

There, you see many adventurers gathered inside.

Once the King appears, he announces that there are rumors that a dragon has left it's cave in the mountains.

And that your quest is go out and find the dragon's treasure, before it returns.

You and the other adventurers, accompanied by the King's Knights, set off towards the mountains.

As you do, you pass through burned forests and charred lands,

With smoke still rising off the ashes...

When you reach the mountains, you hear nothing.

Even as your group ascends, not one sound was made. It seemed as though the dragon had truly left.

After finally reaching the Dragon's cave, you enter, finding a pile of gold and silver, as well as other treasures!

However, just as you do, a messenger from the city finds you!

The messenger informs your group that the dragon has attacked the castle!

The Knights that have accompanied you tell you that everyone must return to the castle and save whatever remains of the Kingdom!

As the rest of your group rushes to leave, you are left with a choice..

Will you save whats left of the kingdom?

Or will you take the treasure for yourself?
